Meet Our Mascot

Published on September 25, 2025

We’re thrilled to introduce you to the heart and spirit of our community — our mascot! More than just a character, this mascot represents everything we stand for — energy, creativity, and togetherness.

Our mascot is designed with inspiration from legendary creatures of old and modern storytelling. The bold colors symbolize courage and ambition, while the playful design reminds us that learning and growing should always be fun.

You’ll start seeing our mascot in events, posts, and campaigns — always cheering you on and making the experience more engaging. Whether it’s a big product launch or a small community meetup, our mascot will be there to spread positivity.

“Mascots create a sense of belonging. They turn a brand into a movement.”

Stay tuned for upcoming interactive sessions where you can share your ideas and even vote on what we should name our mascot! Together, we’ll make this symbol truly ours.
